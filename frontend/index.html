Safe & Transparent Payments

How the money flows.

We use a secure escrow system to ensure every transaction is fair and protected for both parties.

1. Wallet Top-Up

Add funds to your secure Helpido Wallet using Razorpay. Your money is ready whenever you need help.

2. Held in Escrow

When you post a task, the reward is held safely by Helpido. The helper sees the guaranteed payout.

3. Approve & Pay

Once the task is done, you release the funds to the helper. If you forget, funds auto-release after 24 hours.

4. Minimal Fee

Your first 5 requests are 100% free! After that, we take a tiny 7% platform fee only on successful tasks.
